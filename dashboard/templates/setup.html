You're almost ready

Three steps to put Weir between your AI agent and your database.

Step 1
Copy your Docker command
docker run -d \
  -e WEIR_API_KEY={{ api_key }} \
  -e WEIR_TARGET_HOST=host.docker.internal \
  -e WEIR_TARGET_PORT=5432 \
  -p 5433:5433 \
  weir/proxy:latest

host.docker.internal points to your Mac/Windows localhost from inside Docker. On Linux, use your machine's local IP address instead (e.g. 192.168.1.x).

Step 2
Point your agent at port 5433
Before
postgresql://user:pass@localhost:5432/db
→
After
postgresql://user:pass@localhost:5433/db
Step 3
Watch this page

Run a destructive query from your agent — a DELETE, DROP, or UPDATE without a WHERE clause. It will appear here within 2 seconds, held for your approval.
